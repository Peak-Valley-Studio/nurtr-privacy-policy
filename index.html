Peak Valley Studio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Nurtr mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use Nurtr, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password
  • Profile Information: Profile photos, relationship start date, partner information
  • User Content: Photos you upload to the shared gallery, memories and notes you create, calendar events, journal entries, and your chosen couple theme song
  • Relationship Data: Days together counter, milestones, and relationship goals

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features you use, time spent in the app, crash reports, and performance data
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, and app version

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Precise geolocation data
  • Financial or payment information (handled by Apple)
  • Health or medical information
  • Contacts from your device
  • Browsing history outside the App

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

2.1 App Functionality

  • To provide and maintain the App's core features
  • To enable you to connect with your partner
  • To sync your data across your devices
  • To calculate and display your relationship timeline
  • To store and display your shared photos, memories, and calendar events

2.2 Product Improvement

  • To understand how users interact with the App
  • To improve and optimize App performance
  • To develop new features and functionality
  • To fix bugs and technical issues

2.3 Communication

  • To send you important updates about the App
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To send notifications about your relationship milestones (if enabled)

2.4 Security and Compliance

  • To protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To enforce our Terms of Service

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Your Partner

When you connect with your partner in the App, you explicitly share the following information with them:

  • Your profile information (name, photo)
  • Shared gallery photos
  • Shared calendar events
  • Shared memories and notes
  • Relationship goals and milestones

3.2 Service Providers

We use Firebase (Google LLC) as our backend infrastructure provider. Firebase helps us with:

  • Authentication and account management
  • Cloud storage for your photos and data
  • Real-time database synchronization
  • App performance monitoring
  • Crash reporting

Firebase is bound by strict data processing agreements and only processes your data on our behalf. You can read Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

3.4 What We DO NOT Do

  • We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers
  • We do NOT use your data for marketing purposes
  • We do NOT track you across other apps or websites

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Cloud services, which may be located in various countries. Firebase complies with international data protection standards including GDPR.

4.2 How We Protect Your Data

  • Industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Automatic logout after periods of inactivity

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

5.1 Access and Portability

You can access all your personal data within the App. You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at vinicius.deniz@peakvalleystudio.com

5.2 Correction

You can update your profile information and other personal data directly within the App settings.

5.3 Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the App settings. This will permanently delete all your personal data, including:

  • Your account information
  • All uploaded photos
  • All memories and notes
  • All calendar events
  • Your relationship data

Important: If you are connected with a partner, deleting your account will also remove your shared data from their view, but will not delete their account or their personal data.

5.4 Opt-Out of Communications

You can opt out of promotional emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in the email or by adjusting your notification settings in the App.

5.5 Do Not Track

We do not track users across third-party websites or apps. The App does not respond to Do Not Track signals because we do not track users outside of our App.
